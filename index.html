About Sigma Studio

We are a Belgian game development studio focused on immersive multiplayer experiences. Every title we publish carries its own brand and identity — but shares our core philosophy: fair play, top-tier performance, and bold creativity.

Our Game

Our debut title is a high-octane battle royale game, where players compete to be the last one standing on a dynamic island battlefield.

  • Cross-platform multiplayer (PC & console)
  • Seasonal content updates
  • Fully customizable characters & cosmetics
  • Fast-paced, tactical combat

Technology Stack

We use cutting-edge tools to deliver the best player experience:

  • Developed in Unreal Engine 5
  • Cloud-based backend with PlayFab
  • Dedicated high-performance servers
  • Real-time analytics and matchmaking systems

Monetization

Our game is free-to-play, with optional cosmetic purchases that never affect gameplay fairness. Players can unlock additional content through:

  • Seasonal Battle Pass (€9.99)
  • In-game currency bundles
  • Limited-time cosmetic packs
