Our Monetization Model

At Sigma Studio, we believe that great games should be accessible to everyone. That’s why we’ve adopted a fair free-to-play model that respects players while supporting ongoing development and updates.

What We Offer

  • Cosmetic Purchases: Skins, emotes, and visual upgrades that let players express their style — without affecting gameplay balance.
  • Seasonal Battle Pass: A premium track with exclusive rewards, unlocked through challenges and play.
  • In-Game Currency: Optional currency bundles for unlocking cosmetics faster, always fairly priced.

Fair Play Commitment

We never sell items or advantages that affect competitive fairness. Skill, strategy, and teamwork are what define success in our games — not spending power.

Supporting the Future

Revenue from cosmetics and battle passes allows us to invest in new content, community events, server upgrades, and exciting expansions. Every purchase helps build a stronger, better game for everyone.
